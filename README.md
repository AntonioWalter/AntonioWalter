## Projects
- **[VerIta](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/verITA):** I developed a Security and Traceability System for Made in Italy products with my university team, achieving second place at the "Made in Italy Innovation Challenge" hackathon organized by the State Mint and Codemotion. The project addresses the issue of product authenticity by using RFID technology, allowing consumers to verify products through a web app and enhancing trust in Italian manufacturers.
- **[Culturia](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/Culturia):** A web application designed to enhance local cultural heritage through active user participation. The project was born within the "Hackcultura 2025" Hackathon as an innovative proposal to bring everyone closer to culture, promoting accessibility, storytelling, and discovery.
- **[Tree-Time](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/Tree-Time):** An e-commerce platform that allows the purchase of trees to be planted in at-risk areas, contributing to the reduction of CO₂ emissions and supporting vulnerable local communities. Users can track their tree's status, consult their contribution history, and view their positive impact on an interactive map.
- **[Signify](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/Signify):** A real-time American Sign Language (ASL) translation system that bridges the gap between sign language and spoken language. It makes inclusive communication accessible to anyone with a simple webcam, instantly translating signs into natural, structured text.
- **[SINTONIA](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/SINTONIA):** A proactive mental health triage and observation ecosystem. It transforms the patient waitlist experience from passive to dynamic by continuously assessing clinical risk through periodic questionnaires. It provides patients with self-help tools (like mood tracking and diaries) and gives medical professionals dashboards to manage priority queues and automated alerts.
- **[SINTON-IA](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/SINTON-IA):** The Artificial Intelligence module integrated into the SINTONIA mental health ecosystem. It features predictive models to actively support medical professionals by detecting early signs of suicide risk, preventing patient dropout from the platform, and estimating depression states to ensure timely and targeted interventions.
