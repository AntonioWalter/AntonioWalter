## Projects
- **[Tennis Management System](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/tennis-management-system):** This is a project developed to manage professional tennis data using JDBC and MySQL. It demonstrates my knowledge and skills in database management and Java programming.
- **[VerIta](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/verITA):** I developed a Security and Traceability System for Made in Italy products with my university team, achieving second place at the "Made in Italy Innovation Challenge" hackathon organized by the State Mint and Codemotion, which had over 40 participants. The project addresses the issue of product authenticity and traceability by using RFID technology. Each product is equipped with a unique RFID tag, allowing consumers to verify authenticity through a web app. Additionally, we developed a platform for companies to upload and manage their products. This system enhances consumer trust and protects Italian manufacturers from counterfeit products.
- **[Culturia](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/Culturia):** Culturia is a web application designed to enhance local cultural heritage through active user participation.
The project was born within the "Hackcultura 2025" Hackathon as an innovative proposal to bring everyone closer to culture, promoting accessibility, storytelling, and discovery.
- **[Tree-Time](https://github.com/AntonioWalter/Tree-Time):** Tree-Time is a university project developed in 2025 for the Web Software Technologies program at the University of Salerno.
The primary objective is to create an e-commerce platform that allows the purchase of trees—both fruit and non-fruit trees—to be planted in at-risk areas, contributing both to the reduction of CO₂ emissions and to the support of vulnerable local communities.The project balances environmental and social aspects, offering users a transparent and interactive experience.
Every tree purchased is tracked: users can track its status directly on the website, consult their contribution history, and view detailed statistics on their positive impact. Furthermore, an interactive map shows the location of all the trees planted and their owners, ensuring maximum clarity in the planting process.
